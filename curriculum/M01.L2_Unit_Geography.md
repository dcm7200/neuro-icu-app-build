## Seven Spaces You Need Most

### 1. **APP Office**
Where you sit, write notes, decompress. Whiteboard shows TigerConnect role assignments.

### 2. **Attending Office**
Where the daytime attending works. Knock first — family meetings sometimes happen here.

### 3. **Supply Pyxis**
- Cranial access kits
- Ventriculostomy packs
- Central-line trays
- Arterial-line kits
- Lumbar-puncture kits
- Ultrasound-guided IV kits
- Occipital-nerve-block trays

### 4. **Code Blue Room**
Corner of the pod. **ICU Code Blue ≠ Overhead Code Blue** — always confirm overhead is called.

### 5. **EEG/Epilepsy Reading Room**
Walk over here for urgent EEG questions rather than waiting for reports.

### 6. **Conference Rooms**
- 4th-floor Barrow (primary)
- 6th-floor (overflow & didactic)
- Mercy 1st-floor (cross-SJHMC)

### 7. **Barrow Lounge**
Coffee, decompression, shared resource. Bring your own mug.

## Critical Supplies: The 60-Second Drill

Practice this until it's automatic:

**Scenario:** Patient with anisocoria + posturing.  
**Action:** Where's the cranial access kit? **(Pyxis)**

**Scenario:** Code Blue called on Ascom.  
**Action:** Where's the Code Blue room? **(Corner of pod)**  
**Second action:** Is it ICU-only or overhead? **(Confirm overhead activation with charge nurse)**
